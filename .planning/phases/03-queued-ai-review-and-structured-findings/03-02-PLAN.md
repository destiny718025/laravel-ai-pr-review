## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-03-04 | Tampering | provider JSON payload | high | mitigate | Decode with `JSON_THROW_ON_ERROR` and validate the decoded array through `AIReviewPayloadValidator` before any later persistence. |
| T-03-05 | Information Disclosure | safe failure summaries | high | mitigate | `AIReviewFailureMapper` must whitelist human-safe messages and exclude raw provider payload fragments, secrets, and headers per D-15. |
| T-03-06 | Spoofing | provider binding | medium | mitigate | Bind `AIReviewProvider` in the container and keep callers dependent on the contract, not concrete classes. |
| T-03-SC | Tampering | npm/pip/cargo installs | low | accept | No package-manager installs are introduced in this plan; provider and validation use existing Laravel capabilities only. |
</threat_model>
